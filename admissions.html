Admission Process

Simple steps for parent enquiry.

1. Submit Enquiry

Fill the admission enquiry form with student and parent details.

2. School Interaction

The office can contact parents for class availability, visit timing, and documents.

3. Document Submission

Submit required documents and complete the admission formalities.

4. Confirmation

Admission confirmation is provided after review and completion of school requirements.
