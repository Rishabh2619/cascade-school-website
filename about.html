About Cascade International School

A school profile page built for trust, clarity, and a strong first impression for parents.

Our Story

Building confident learners since 2007.

Cascade International School is located in Bahadrabad, Haridwar and presents a balanced learning environment for children. The school website highlights academics, facilities, values, and admissions in a simple parent-friendly format.

The content is intentionally clear and easy to update, so official phone numbers, staff messages, real photos, and notices can be added quickly before final launch.

V

Vision

To nurture responsible, confident, and capable learners who are ready for the future.

M

Mission

To provide quality education through discipline, practical learning, and holistic development.

C

Core Values

Respect, curiosity, discipline, teamwork, integrity, and care for the community.

Principal's Message

Education is most effective when children feel guided, challenged, and supported. Our aim is to create an environment where students learn with discipline, ask questions with confidence, and grow with strong values.
